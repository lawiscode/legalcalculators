What is this?

This is NOT legal advise. Consult an attorney if you need legal advice.

This site uses data from the National Inventory of Collateral Consequences, data from the MA Sentencing Commission, and an analysis of aggregate vistor interactions to provide a user-tailored ordering of collateral consequences triggered by MA criminal convictions. For more information, visit our wiki.
